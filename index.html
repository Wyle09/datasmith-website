Solutions

DataSmith has many software solutions to help add value to your business and to enable efficiencies in your operation.

Total Compensation Statements

Inform your employees about the benefits you provide them. Let DataSmith design a professional compensation statement for your employees. Help your employees understand the additional value that your company provides them. Let them consider their savings potential and inform them of important benefit and savings.

Performance Management and Compensation Planning

DataSmith can optimize your performance management and compensation review process. Let DataSmith's automated system add efficiency and process to your performance management and compensation review process. The DataSmith solution includes a template system that allows you to configure the system to meet your process.

Data Warehousing

Your HR data is one of your most important assets. Let DataSmith help you leverage this information by putting it at your fingertips with an easy to understand data model that takes data from multiple sources and combines it in an easy to understand format. This data can be easily accessed via web based tools or traditional report writing packages. We have standard templates and connectors for Microsoft SQL Reporting Services, Crystal Reports and Microsoft Excel.
